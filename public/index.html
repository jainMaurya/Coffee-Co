OUR STORY

Established in the year 2000, Coffee & Co. Company is the pioneer of coffee culture in India. The Coffee & Co. chain delivers a true coffee experience in a warm, friendly, and relaxed environment. It provides a comfortable place for people to unwind over interesting conversations and a hot cup of coffee. Besides India, Our cafés are located across the islands of Sri Lanka and Maldives.

ICEBREAKER GAMES

At Coffee & Co., we believe that great conversations are the cornerstone of building meaningful relationships. That’s why we’ve introduced our exclusive collection of Icebreaker Games, designed to spark engaging dialogues between friends, strangers, or even first-time visitors.

Whether it’s a lighthearted trivia game, a fun social challenge, or a brain-teasing puzzle, there’s something for everyone.With every sip of coffee, the games aim to bridge gaps and create memories that last.
